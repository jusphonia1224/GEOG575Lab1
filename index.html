Distribution of COVID-19 Import Cases to Hong Kong SAR

About the Map

This map is aimed to display the distribution of COVID-19 import cases through flight, trains or ships from other cities or countries to Hong Kong SAR between 1 Oct 2022 and 14 Oct 2022.

Distribution of import cases could provide a picture for assisting us in preparing precaution kits before travelling.

Data are retrieved from DATA.GOV.HK with the list of flights/trains/ships/vehicles taken by cases tested positive for SARS-CoV-2 virus in the past 7 days.
